## Racing Concepts

### Line Types
- **Optimal/Fastest:** Late apex, maximum time
- **Defensive:** Inside line, protect position
- **Overtaking:** Different from optimal, sets up pass

### Sectors
- Track divided into 2-3 timed sections
- Identify where you're fast/slow
- Focus practice on weak sectors

### Optimal vs. PB
- **PB (Personal Best):** Your fastest single lap
- **Theoretical Optimal:** Best S1 + Best S2 + Best S3
- **Gap** = Potential improvement available

```

- PB: 1:29.691
```
