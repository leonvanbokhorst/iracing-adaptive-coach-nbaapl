## Why This Split Matters

**The Problem:**
Original Chapter 15 was 724 lines of setup theory. But ~500 lines were about adjustments you CAN'T MAKE in fixed setup!

**The Solution:**
- **Fixed Setup Guide:** What matters NOW (brake bias, driver vs. setup)
- **Open Setup Reference:** Full theory for LATER (when you need it)

**The Philosophy:**
Don't study what you can't use. Focus on what you CAN control: **your driving**.

In fixed setup racing:
- Everyone has the same car
- Faster drivers = better technique, not better setup
- Your edge comes from DRIVING, not tweaking
