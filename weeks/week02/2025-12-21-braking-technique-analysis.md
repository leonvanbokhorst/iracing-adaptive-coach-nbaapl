## üéì Key Lessons Learned

### 1. üö® **HARDWARE Changes Have Consequences** (The Big One)

- Changed throttle travel + spring ‚Üí unconscious foot position changed
- Resting foot on throttle = 10-20% input during braking
- **THREE conflicting inputs** (brake + throttle + steering) = chaos
- Hardware changes require conscious adaptation, not just recalibration

### 2. **"Feel" Matches Data Perfectly**

- Felt "disconnected" ‚Üí data shows inconsistent technique + throttle overlap
- Felt "snap oversteer" ‚Üí data shows brake + throttle + steering overload in S3
- Felt "fighting the car" ‚Üí car WAS fighting itself (conflicting pedal inputs)
- Instinct was Right: "Like some stress reaction... not relaxed"

### 3. **Throttle Overlap Breaks EVERYTHING**

- No amount of "commitment" or "backing off" fixes conflicting pedal inputs
- Brake effectiveness reduced (throttle fighting it)
- Weight transfer confused (forward + backward simultaneously)
- Rear tire stability destroyed (drive + brake + cornering = overload)
- **Must fix foot position BEFORE working on any other technique**

### 4. **"Backing Off" Can Mean Different Things**

- Backing off Commitment = can work (controlled aggression)
- Backing off COMMITMENT + throttle overlap = disaster
- Must have Clean inputs first, then adjust aggression level

### 5. **Inconsistency Kills Performance**

- One corner tentative, one corner aggressive = no rhythm
- Flow requires UNIFIED approach across entire lap
- Can't have unified approach with unconscious throttle overlap

### 6. **The Traction Circle Is Real (Now With THREE Inputs)**

- Can't brake hard AND turn hard simultaneously
- Definitely can't brake + turn + throttle simultaneously!
- Must sequence inputs: throttle Off ‚Üí brake ‚Üí turn ‚Üí brake off ‚Üí throttle ON
- Overloading tires = sliding = snap oversteer

### 7. **Desperation Braking Is A Symptom**

- When you brake WAY harder late in lap = trying to recover earlier losses
- Fix: Don't lose time early (by having clean inputs), don't need to recover late

### 8. **Diagnosis Requires Multiple Data Sources**

- Brake pressure analysis alone = incomplete picture
- Throttle/brake overlay graph = revealed root cause
- Visual data (Garage 61) caught what numeric analysis missed
- Always check ALL inputs, not just one
