## Challenges üöß

**1. The Commitment Paradox (Dec 21 PM)**  
Tried to "back off 5%" but backed off in wrong places: softer braking, less acceleration, but 40% MORE steering. Result: sliding instead of gripping. Lesson: 95% pace means 95% brake PRESSURE (still hard), 95% steering INPUT (smoother), not backing off commitment.

**2. Throttle Overlap Hardware Issue (Dec 21 PM)**  
Recent pedal changes ‚Üí unconscious foot resting at 10-20% throttle during braking. Three conflicting inputs = confused weight transfer = snap oversteer. Required conscious "FOOT UP" drill.

**3. Impulse Control When Position Secured (Dec 22 PM)**  
When goal is ACTIVE (catch P3), strategic/smart/fast. When goal is ACHIEVED (P3 secured), ADHD brain creates artificial pressure ‚Üí impulse overrides strategy ‚Üí unnecessary risk ‚Üí spin.

**4. Overdriving in Qualifying (Dec 22 PM)**  
Qualified P10 (1:30.153) by trying too hard. Same Jefferson pattern: trying too hard = slower. Need pre-quali calm routine.

**5. Mental Spiral from Multiple Incidents (Dec 22 AM)**  
Can recover from ONE incident perfectly. But TWO incidents in close succession create mental disruption. Need "Incident = Survival Lap" protocol.
