## The Track

Short lap, long workload. Bumps into T3 and T10. Off-camber traps that punish greedy entries. Crosswinds across the infield that move your braking points by meters. The kind of place that rewards discipline and punishes overdriving.

In the Ray FF1600: a **discipline and traction exam** disguised as a tiny club circuit. Just enough power that every poor exit hurts. Not nearly enough to ignore the mistakes.
